Overview:

Sales Pitch:

Recipe-App is an app that allows you to have your grocery and recipe lists all in one place. It includes features such as:

  • Adding recipes
  • Deleting recipes
  • Editing recipes
  • Searching recipes
  • Categories
  • Bookmarking
  • Quick adding to the grocery list
  • A simple interface
  • No frills, just what you need

Postmortem:

What went right in the development process? :

As a group, we had the necessary skills to create what we wanted to. Should one of us not possess a skill,
at least one other member would have the necessary knowledge. This means that aside from any features we didn’t have time for,
we were able to implement everything we wanted to with not too many issues.

What went wrong in the development process? :

Despite the fact that we had the skills, we did not manage our time well. There were many days
when we did not communicate with each other and because of this, we didn’t know if work was
being done. Because of this, we tended to leave work to the last minute which was the problem
we wanted to solve in our iteration 2 retrospective. Unfortunately, we didn’t follow up with our
plan and as such, we ran into time constraints and had to cut a few features out.

Velocity:

We cannot get a velocity chart, but we can estimate what it would look like.
It would likely appear to have many spikes in velocity as the milestone deadlines approach,
this is because we didn’t manage our time very well and because of this,
We spent a lot of time doing work close to the deadline.
estimated to spent 3 days and 6 hours and we ended up spending 4 days and 2 hours.
This is different is high and it is evidence that we need to work on time management.
